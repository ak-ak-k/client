How it works

Let’s get you

Started on Refr

Discover & Shop from stores listed on the Refr marketplace Shop from a wide variety of categories listed on Refr ranging from products & services and stores which are online, offline or both.

SHOP

  • Visit a store partnered with Refr, complete a minimum purchase.
  • While checking out, scan the Refr QR code which lets you directly share your experience at the store with your circle on chat or social.
  • Make sure to add a couple of lines about your experience before sending out the recommendation, the recommendation curation page lets you do just that.
  • Get rewarded with an instant cashback for spreading the word for the store.
  • When your friends use your recommendation to visit the same store, they get rewarded & so do you for every successful referral.

ONLINE

  • Simply click ‘Refr’ after placing the order & share your shopping experience at the store with your circle on various platforms.
  • Your cashback will be credited once the product is delivered & accepted.

ONLINE + OFFLINE

You can either visit the physical store or order products online from these businesses. In both cases, you will receive cashback on your purchase after you have recommended it to your friends.
