How it works

Vocal

for Local

You have previously visited these stores & shopped from them. These brands value you as a customer and give you an opportunity to earn on your past purchases. You can recommend these stores from the comfort of your house or on the go.. let friends know about your experience with the products or services of the brand and earn every time your recommendation is used.

All of this, without even making a purchase. So, you get to earn cashbacks even when you have not spent any money!!

How does this work?

  • You will receive advocacy messages from brands on your chat or social platforms.
  • Be on the look out for Refr Qr codes on online product packages either in form of packaging stickers or cards.
  • Click the link in the message to save the advocacy in your Refr app under the ‘Get Vocal for your Favourite Stores’ section.
  • You can also receive in app messages from various brands in the same section..so always keep your eyes on this for endless earning opportunities.
  • From here, open the store card of your choice & simply click ‘Refr’ to share with your friends.
  • You earn cashback on the go every time your friends purchase from these stores using your recommendation, which gets credited in your Refr wallet.

Pro tip

There is no limit to how much you can earn. The more you share, the more chances you have of earning!!
