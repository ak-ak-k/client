How it works

Cashback 2X

with friends recommendations

Just like you send out store recommendations, you will also receive recommendations from your friends, family or followers on various chat & social platforms. These are genuine recommendations of products & services your friends have used & loved, helping you discover their favourite stores. They help increase your trust in a purchase, while minimizing your browsing time and exchanges.

Click on the link in these messages to access these recommendations, you can either choose to use them right away or save them for use later. These will then get saved in the ‘Stores Recommended by Friends’ section, from where you can use them whenever you want & get rewarded on your purchase.

To purchase via a recommendation

  • From the ‘Stores Recommended by Friends’ section on home, open the store card you want to purchase from.
  • Click on ‘Get this’ at the bottom of the recommended store card.

For an offline store

You have to visit the physical location of the store. Once you are at the store, please inform the storefront that you will be redeeming a recommendation on Refr. After you have chosen the product or taken the service, tick the box that says “I’m at the store” & then click ‘Pay & Redeem’ at the storefront of the store….that’s it! You now receive a cashback on your purchase, & so does your friend who recommended the store to you. You can also now Refr the business to your friends, family & followers to continue earning cashbacks every time they use your recommendation.

For an online store

  • After you have clicked ‘Get this’ on the recommended store card, you can select from a wide variety of products the store has to offer from its catalogue.
  • Complete a purchase online & you will get a cashback once your product is delivered. It’s as simple as that.

Pro tip

Thank your friends for this! Friends’ recommendations come with maximum benefits. You earn double cashback when you shop using a friend’s recommendation.
