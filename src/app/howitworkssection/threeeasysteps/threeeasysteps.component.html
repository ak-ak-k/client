How it works

3 Easy Steps

Shop

Share

Earn

Refr is a marketplace where shopping is Fun, Interactive and Rewarding. A more connected shopping experience. Here you can discover, shop & send out recommendations for your favourite brands, products & services to your friends, family and followers and help them discover stuff you like..and get rewarded for the same.

SHOP

You take a service or buy a product, either by visiting the store or online through the Refr app.

SHARE

While checking out, you send a recommendation of the store to your circle on chat or social.

EARN

You get rewarded with a cashback instantly for doing this. When your friends use your recommendation, they earn a cashback & so do you!

Shop smarter with Refr

When you receive recommendations from your friends, you discover cool stores around you that are tried & tested by them which minimizes your chances of making a wrong purchase. What can be better than this? You earn higher cashbacks than your friends when you use their recommendations! All these cashbacks are in the form of Refr cash.
