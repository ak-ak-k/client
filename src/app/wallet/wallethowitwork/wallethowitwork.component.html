Refr cash is the in-app currency you receive on Refr

You earn cashbacks in the form of Refr cash when you shop online or in store on the Refr Marketplace

You can spend the collected Refr cash across listed stores of all categories & on specially curated deals in the Burn section

Refr cash can be used to make a part or full payment of an order

Refr cash cannot be transferred to your bank account
