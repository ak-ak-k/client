How To Redeem

You can either visit the store or make an online purchase

For an In-store purchase, make sure you show the frontdesk to verify before you hit the redeem button

For an online purchase, make your selection of products and proceed to checkout

Earn instant cashback In-store or get cashback once your product is delivered and accepted

Terms and conditions

  1. In-store cashback must be confirmed by the store. Make sure to show the frontdesk that you have shared the store's Refr campaign with your friends before you hit the Redeem button. The cashier will then approve and an instant cashback will be reflected in your Refr wallet.
  2. Multiple codes of a store cannot be redeemed on a single bill.
  3. After the expiry date of a campaign, you cannot redeem the Refr code received from a friend at the store.
  4. For online purchases, cashback will be credited once the product is delivered and accepted.
  5. Cashback is credited as Refr cash to your wallet which can be spent on all stores listed on the Refr app or even withdrawn to your linked wallets.
  6. Cashback will not be credited if any transaction or delivery with the store is taking place outside the Refr app.
  7. Refr codes will not be applicable alongside any ongoing offers the store is running.
  8. Any returns, refunds or exchanges will adhere to the policies of the store from where the product has been ordered.
